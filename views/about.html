About Us

About GenesisBlock

We are the world’s first alternative cryptocurrency options exchange. Our platform also comes with a regular exchange to swap cryptocurrencies. All options contracts are priced in terms of bitcoin, so there is absolutely no fiat currency involved. Thus, anyone can get started trading right away without any additional fees or setup time. All options are American-style options, so you can exercise at any time.

Typical Use Cases

  • There are numerous reasons why stock exchanges and derivative markets exist. GenesisBlock provides traders a unique opportunity to utilize advanced financial instruments for a specific purpose. The list below shows some common usage scenarios.
  • Scrypt miners, or other miners that are willing to pay a premium as insurance against a possible future decrease in price for the specified asset.
  • Anyone holding altcoins that want to earn a premium.
  • Traders who want to speculate on the Bitcoin to altcoin rate. Buying/selling options contracts can be more efficient than trading on the spot market, and offers effective leverage. A trader can more effectively take long or short positions, or take bets on volatility.
  • Traders who want to arbitrage and make the market. Trading options contracts provides perfect possibility to arbitrage between spot market and options market. Everyone is able to provide liquidity on the market on equal conditions.
  • Algorithmic traders. GenesisBlock allows traders to build complex strategies and test them in real time without the need to connect to a specific broker or pay additional fees.
  • We will eventually scale to more effectively accomodate high-frequency trading. Though HFT's impact on the market is being constantly debated, GenesisBlock won't prohibit high-frequency trading as long as it does not interfere usual human/bot trading.
